Press Kit

Hailing from the hills of Northwest Arkansas, Coming Up Aces is a four-piece classic rock band forged from friendship and a shared love of the classics. Drawing deep from the well of Southern rock legends like Lynyrd Skynyrd and the heartland storytelling of Tom Petty, then running it through the raw, unfiltered energy of grunge, Coming Up Aces has carved out a sound that feels both timeless and alive.
